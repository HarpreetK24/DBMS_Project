About Us

Jyoti Nivas College is founded by the Sisters of St. Joseph's of Tarbes. Since its inception in 1966, Jyoti Nivas College has remained steadfast to one central commitment- to provide a balanced, comprehensive education in Arts, Science and Commerce, aimed at turning out intellectually enlightened, morally upright, spiritually oriented and socially committed young persons. It strives to nurture and enhance the innate wealth of talent and potential within each student encouraging everyone to truly live the motto of the college. Our Motto "Let Your Light Shine" Our Vision "Communion, Excellence, Service, Relevance"
